Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?

Yes! The Google Ads Quality Checker is completely free for all users. No credit card required, no hidden fees. We provide this tool to help businesses improve their Google Ads performance.

Do I need to create an account?

You can run a one-time analysis without an account. However, creating a free account allows you to save your reports, track performance over time, and access additional features.

What data do you access?

We only request read-only access to your Google Ads account. We never make changes to your campaigns, and we don't share your data with third parties. You can revoke access at any time from your Google account settings.

How is the score calculated?

Your overall score is a weighted average of 10+ performance dimensions including Quality Score, CTR, wasted spend, ad optimization, mobile performance, and more. Each section is scored against industry benchmarks and best practices.

Can I run multiple reports?

Absolutely! You can run reports as often as you like. We recommend running a new analysis monthly to track your improvements over time.
