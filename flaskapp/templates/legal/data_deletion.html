Data Deletion for Facebook Login

If you signed in with Facebook and want to delete your data from our systems, you have two options:

  1. Use Facebook’s in-app “Delete Your Data” flow: Open your Facebook settings → Apps and Websites → locate our app → select Remove / Delete your data. Facebook will send us an authorized request and we’ll process it automatically.
  2. Contact us directly: Email support@yourdomain.com with the subject “Facebook Data Deletion Request.” Please include the email or user ID you used with Facebook Login.

What happens next?

We will delete or anonymize account-linked data (e.g., access tokens, profile mappings, and activity records) associated with your Facebook user ID. Deletions are processed promptly and typically complete within 30 days.

If you initiated the request through Facebook, you may be shown a confirmation link that points to our deletion status page under /account/facebook/data_deletion_status/<code>.

For details on how we handle data generally, see our Privacy Policy.
