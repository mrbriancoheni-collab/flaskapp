Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. We'll prorate any changes automatically.
Do I really not need a credit card for the free trial?
Absolutely not. We want you to experience the value first. Just sign up with your email, connect your marketing channels, and start seeing results. No credit card required until you decide to continue after your 14-day trial.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation. During this time, you can reactivate your account or export your historical performance data. After 30 days, your account is permanently deleted for security and privacy.
Which marketing channels do you support?
We integrate with Google Ads, Google Local Services Ads (LSA), Facebook Ads, Google Business Profile, Google Analytics, and Google Search Console. More channels are being added regularly based on customer requests.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with the results within your first 30 days of paid subscription, we'll refund your payment in full. No questions asked.
How does the AI actually help my business?
